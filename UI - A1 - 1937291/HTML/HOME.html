About Us

The Developer Club is designed to meet the expectations and interests of those passionate about technology, programming and working as a team. We aim to create our own software, websites, games and strive to engage ourself in the progressive world of technologies. We hope to peak the interests of all those fascinated with the industry, and if you find yourself interested in any of these things, please feel free to enlist today!

Events

As of this month, since the semester is only just starting, we don't have many events officially planned. However, if you are interested in taking an active roll within Developer Club, please feel free to join our Discord server and we will make sure to assign you a role. Just let us know what you're interested in!

Meetings

Since we understand that everyone has busy schedules because of academics, we have weekly online meetings! Please be aware that all of the meetings will be taking place on discord so if interested, feel free to enlist now! We look forward to working with you! Schedule can be found on our discord server!
